🏗️ Building

I'm building out this personal website and blog — writing about SEO, digital marketing, web development, and what it's like being a digital native in 2026. Every page is hand-coded HTML and CSS, deployed on Cloudflare Pages. No frameworks, no bloat.

💼 Working

I'm working in digital marketing and SEO here in San Diego. My focus is on helping businesses get found online — through technical SEO, content strategy, local search optimization, and smart use of AI tools in marketing workflows.

🤖 Exploring

AI-powered content workflows are my current obsession. I'm experimenting with how AI tools can fit into real marketing workflows — not as a replacement for human creativity, but as an accelerator. From drafting to editing to repurposing, there's a lot of untapped potential.
