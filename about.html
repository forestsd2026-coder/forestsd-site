Philosophy

I believe in shipping over planning, learning by doing, and being direct. The best way to understand something is to build it. The best way to communicate is to skip the preamble and say what you mean.

I'm not precious about tools or frameworks. Whatever gets the job done well, that's what I use. Python for most things, JavaScript when needed, bash when it's quick, and a browser when nothing else works.
