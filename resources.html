Resources

Tools and platforms I use and recommend for digital work

🔍 SEO Tools

Google Search Console

Free, essential. Monitor your site's search performance, indexing status, and Core Web Vitals directly from Google.

Screaming Frog

The gold standard for technical SEO audits. Crawl your site to find broken links, duplicate content, and redirect chains.

Schema Markup Generator

Tools like Merkle's schema generator or Schema.dev make creating JSON-LD structured data quick and error-free.

Ahrefs / Semrush

Keyword research, backlink analysis, competitor intelligence. Pick one (or both) — they're invaluable for any SEO workflow.

Google Keyword Planner

Free keyword research straight from Google Ads. Great for search volume estimates and discovering new keyword ideas.

✍️ Content Creation

Claude / ChatGPT

AI writing assistants for brainstorming, drafting, editing, and research. Best used as a collaborative tool, not a replacement.

Midjourney / DALL-E

AI image generation for blog graphics, social media visuals, and creative concepts. Incredibly fast from idea to visual.

Descript

Video and podcast editing made simple. Edit video by editing text — perfect for repurposing content across formats.

Canva

Quick, professional graphic design without needing Photoshop. Templates for social posts, presentations, and more.

💻 Web Development

Cloudflare Pages

Free static site hosting with global CDN, automatic HTTPS, and git-based deploys. What this site runs on.

GitHub

Version control, collaboration, and CI/CD. The backbone of modern development workflows.

VS Code

The code editor. Lightweight, extensible, and free. Paired with Copilot, it's an incredibly productive environment.

Hugo / Eleventy / Astro

Static site generators for when you need more structure than raw HTML but want to keep things fast and simple.

📊 Analytics

Google Analytics 4

The industry standard for web analytics. Event-based tracking, audience insights, and conversion measurement.

Cloudflare Web Analytics

Privacy-first, free analytics. No cookies, no tracking scripts. Lightweight and built into Cloudflare's dashboard.

Plausible / Fathom

Privacy-friendly GA alternatives. Simple dashboards, GDPR-compliant, and respect your visitors' data.

Looker Studio

Free dashboarding from Google. Connect Search Console, GA4, and other data sources for custom reports.

📣 Marketing

Beehiiv / ConvertKit

Modern email newsletter platforms. Great for building audiences, automations, and monetizing content.

Buffer / Hootsuite

Social media scheduling and management. Plan content across platforms and track engagement from one place.

HubSpot CRM

Free CRM for managing contacts, deals, and marketing pipelines. Scales from solo to enterprise.

Zapier / Make

Automation platforms that connect your tools. Trigger workflows between email, CRM, social, and hundreds of apps.
